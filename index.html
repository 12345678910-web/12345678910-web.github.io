The AI Physical Safety & Home Privacy Act

Ensuring a safe future for humanoid robotics in our homes.

What Problem Does This Solve?

With the rise of robots like Figure 03 and Mythos, our private lives are being mapped by vision-language-action models. Currently, there is no federal law protecting you from "data leakage" inside your bedroom or bathroom, nor clear rules on who pays when a robot breaks something expensive.

Main Rules of the Law

  • On-Device Only: Sensitive home data cannot leave the robot's hardware without explicit, recurring consent.
  • Physical Kill-Switch: All humanoid robots must have a physical emergency stop button.
  • Manufacturer Accountability: Companies are liable for damages caused by AI reasoning errors.

Who Benefits?

Families, privacy-conscious citizens, and the elderly who rely on AI assistance but deserve safety and dignity.

Possible Criticisms

"This will stifle innovation and make US-based robotics companies less competitive against international firms with fewer restrictions."

Why Support It?

We shouldn't have to choose between cutting-edge help and our fundamental right to privacy. The PSHP Act ensures that the AI revolution serves humans, rather than exploiting them.
