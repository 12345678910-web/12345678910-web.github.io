Protect Privacy in the Age of AI

The AI Physical Safety & Home Privacy Act ensures technology works for you—not against you.

The Problem

Home robots are getting smarter—but also more invasive. Without regulation, sensitive data from your home can be collected, stored, or misused with little accountability.

Main Protections

🔒 Data Stays Local

Your private home data cannot leave your device without permission.

⛔ Kill Switch

Every robot must include a physical emergency shutdown.

⚖️ Accountability

Companies are responsible for damages caused by AI mistakes.

Why It Matters

You shouldn’t trade your privacy for convenience. This law protects families, the elderly, and everyday users from hidden risks in AI-powered homes.
